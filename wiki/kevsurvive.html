Kevcore SMP Wiki

Kevcore SMP was the second server to be made, and is still running to this day. Kevcore SMP is also known as KevSurvive or KCSMP.

KCSMP is the original server to be played on. It has a variety of fun stuff made using commands, such as custom enchantments, currency, and more all made using commands in Vanilla minecraft. There is also a datapack you can download and put into your world (in the downloads page).

Custom Enchantments

There are a lot of Custom Enchantments. All Custom Enchantments have a Pros and Cons.

Here are the list of the Custom Enchantments and their pros and cons

ENCHANTMENT PROS CONS
Auto Smelt Turns ores into ingot and Debris into Netherite Netherrack turns into Nether Brick (bad for farming blocks)
Fire Maker Makes fire at the location the arrow is hit Kills the arrow on hit
Lava Walker Turns Lava into cobblestone, the same way as Frost Walker Glitchy (I made it so you lag)
Ice Aspect Slows enemies down Some mobs can make you slow
